House of Culture

Nestled in the heart of Cojusna, Republic of Moldova, this concept envisions a House of Culture that serves as a dynamic hub for artistic expression, community engagement, and the celebration of local heritage.

The soul of the design is rooted in preserving and revitalizing the historic elements of the house while infusing it with contemporary elements, creating a harmonious and unique aesthetic that tells a story of both the house's storied past and its promising future.
